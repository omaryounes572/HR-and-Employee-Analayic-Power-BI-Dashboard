# 🧑‍💼 Employee Attrition & HR Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Overview
An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing employee demographics, job satisfaction, compensation, and work environment to uncover patterns behind attrition.  
This project provides HR teams with insights to reduce employee turnover, improve retention strategies, and enhance workplace satisfaction.

---

## 🛠 Key Steps
1. **Data Cleaning** – Removed duplicates, handled missing values, standardized formats, and cleaned categorical fields.
2. **Feature Engineering** – Created calculated fields like years in company categories, satisfaction score groups, and attrition rate.
3. **Data Analysis** – Examined KPIs such as total employees, attrition count, attrition rate, average satisfaction, and average salary.
4. **Visualization** – Built multiple charts for demographics, compensation patterns, promotion timelines, job satisfaction, and attrition reasons.
5. **Filters** – Added slicers for department, gender, job role, marital status, and education.

---

## 🎯 Outcome
- Identified high-risk attrition groups by age, department, and job role.
- Discovered correlation between low job satisfaction and high turnover.
- Provided recommendations for salary adjustments, promotion policies, and work-life balance improvements.
- Enabled HR managers to create targeted retention plans.

---

## 💡 Tools Used
- **Microsoft POWER BI** – Data cleaning, analysis, and dashboard creation.
- **Color Hunt** – Professional color scheme for visuals.
